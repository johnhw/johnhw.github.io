# DCS-2002: Silver graduation anniversary On July 9th 2027 it will have been 25 years since the Computing Science/Software Engineering graduating class of 1998-2002 at the University of Glasgow graduated. ![The Boyd Orr Level 4 lab, in 2002.](imgs/01010010.jpg) We are considering organising a reunion to celebrate this milestone and to catch up with old friends and indeed long-forgotten classmates. This is a placeholder to gauge interest in such an event. If you are a member of the class of 2002, please fill in the form below to let us know if you would be interested in attending a reunion in Summer 2027. * [Register your interest in a reunion event.](https://forms.office.com/e/mnxzmsQC93) **Please pass this on to anyone you know who was in the class of 2002.** We are trying to reach as many people as possible, but we don't have contact details for very many people yet. ![It was all such a blur...](imgs/07090028.jpg) If you are interesting in helping organise the event, [contact John](mailto:johnh.williamson@glasgow.ac.uk).
